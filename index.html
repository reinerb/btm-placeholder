B'nai Torah MetroWest

B'nai Torah MetroWest invites you to join us as we embark on a new journey together!

Affectionately known as “BTM,” we are a Reformed, pluralistic, inclusive, community located in the Western suburbs of Boston.

At the heart of who we are is a deep commitment to social action and a hope to build a community partnership that will let each of us take responsibility for our own Judaism in a way that feels right.

All Are Welcome!

We welcome, with open arms, multi-faith families as well as persons of all races, ethnicities, genders, and sexualities.

The Annual Sustaining Pledge is $1,800 per family.
